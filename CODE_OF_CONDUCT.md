# Code of Conduct

WannaBuild is a small project that wants to stay a good place to build. This
applies in every project space — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any
other contribution surface.

## Our standards

- Be kind and assume good faith.
- Welcome newcomers; critique ideas, not people.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully, and give it generously.
- No harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or other unprofessional
  conduct.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject any contribution, and may warn or ban
anyone whose behavior they judge inappropriate.

To report a concern, use a private channel rather than a public issue. Contact
the maintainers via <https://github.com/gl11tchy> and request a private channel
(the same path described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for sensitive reports).
Reports are handled confidentially.

This Code of Conduct is adapted, in spirit, from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
